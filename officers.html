Officers

Ryan Vo

Greetings everyone, I’ll be your president for the 2016-2017 school year. I’m responsible for making sure that the administrative side of things, such as room reservations and planning meetings, runs smoothly so that everyone can watch anime in club without problems. During weekly screenings, you can find me chatting with members or discussing matters with the other officers.

I enjoy watching shows that fall under the romance, comedy, and slice-of-life umbrella. Some examples of the above are my personal favorites, Sakurasou no Pet na Kanojo, Clannad, and Nichijou.
My main interest lies in reading visual novels, as the club’s resident VN enthusiast. I particularly love all of Key’s works for their touching tales of friendship and family, having read everything released from Clannad and forward. Out of all of the visual novels that I’ve read, Little Busters! is by far the most compelling with the characters’ struggle to find trust in their friendships.
Finally, the last of my hobbies lies in collecting otaku merchandise, mostly in the realm of Key <of course> and Madoka Magica-related things.

Chris Ozawa

Hi friends, GBM Leader here! In a nutshell, that means I'll probably be the person explaining anime showing stuff like why such and such anime is worth voting for and encouraging GBM activity since it's important to me that members get to know each other and bond over discussing anime and the like.

As far as what kind of series I enjoy, it's hard to pick a specific genre. Broadly speaking I really gravitate toward character dramas <White Album 2, Kokoro Connect>, deconstructions <Re:Zero, Evangelion>, and battle of wits <Liar Game, Kaiji>. Overall though, my favorite anime would have to be Gurren Lagann as a high-energy, inspiring tale of one boy's growth and his fights against all odds that has resonated with me for many years. I thoroughly enjoy discussing anime themes, development, and the like, and am prone to writing walls of text about these things if I'm not careful. If you want me to give a series a shot or feel like talking about something you're watching, I'm all ears and I'd definitely like to hear your thoughts.
